## Grading rubric for the outcome statement:
3 marks if All outcomes statements meet criteria
2 marks if Most outcomes statements meet these criteria:
1 mark if Few outcomes statements meet these criteria:
0 marks if No outcomes statements meet these criteria:

1. Result. The statement contains a type of result.
2. End of JTBD. The outcome is a result the customer wants when the Job to be done is finished.
3. Directional. The outcome indicates a direction of improvement (e.g. increase, minimize)
4. Measurable. There’s a way to determine that the outcome was achieved. The specific,
measured result may or may not be in the Outcome statement.
5. Accurate. The outcome does not include the persona’s name, use case, JTBD, product or
feature.
