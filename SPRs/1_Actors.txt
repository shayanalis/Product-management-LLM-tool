- Actor: Category of users interacting with a product.\n- Role in "Job to be Done" framework: Defines problem space domain.\n- Excludes: Organizations, Physical entities.\n- Focus: Individual\'s challenges with product.\n- Perspective: User\'s and business\'s.\n- Actor Examples: "Rider" for Public Transportation, "Fan" for Sporting Events, "Cardholder" for Payment processing, "Shopper" for Online shopping. \n- Actor Scope: Can range from general (all teachers) to specific (college teachers at specific room).\n- Avoid: Overly broad or narrow actor definition.\n- Alternate Terminology: "Customer," "Customer Persona," "User Persona," "Persona," "Customer Archetype," "User Archetype."\n- Course Consistency: Use "actor.
